📜 How to Play

🎯 Your Mission

You are a Prompt Wizard! Your magic spells are prompts — instructions you give an AI. Better spells = more powerful results!

🗺️ Quest Map

5 dungeons to conquer, each teaching one spell ingredient. Complete them in order. Score 60+ to clear a dungeon and earn XP!

🔮 The 5 Spell Ingredients

🎭 Role — who the AI should be
🎯 Task — what you want
📖 Context — background info
📏 Format — how to show it
📐 Constraint — any limits

📊 How Feedback Works

After every spell you get: ✅ what you did well, ⚠️ what is missing, and 💡 exact fixes so you know how to improve. Try again as many times as you want!

💡 Pro tip: After reading your feedback, hit "Try Again" and rewrite your prompt using the suggestions. Watch your score jump!
