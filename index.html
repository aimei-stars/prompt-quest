How to Play

🎯 Your Mission

You are a Prompt Wizard! Your magic spells are called prompts — instructions you give to an AI. The better your spell, the more powerful the result!

🔮 The 5 Spell Parts

Great prompts are made of ingredients, just like potions:

🎭 Role — who the AI should be
🎯 Task — what you want
📖 Context — background info
⚙️ Format — how to show it
📏 Constraint — any limits

🗺️ Quest Map

There are 5 dungeons to conquer, each teaching one spell ingredient. Complete them in order to unlock the boss challenge. Earn XP and level up your wizard!

💡 Pro tip: Vague spells get vague results. Specific spells create MAGIC! After each attempt, you can see AI feedback and try to improve your score.
